Do you know where your Children are?

It's 10:00 pm and you have already sent 3 texts to your son. You hate that he doesn't ever care to respond on time. Should you keep trying? Or just give up? If only there was an easier way to communicate with you child...

In comes TenPm.

With TenPm, parents can hold their children accountable right from their computer. With the press of a button a parent can send an inquiry to their child that will ask for a reply with both their location and state of health. All the child needs to do is pick up their phone, go to messages, click on the automatically generated link, and enter in a previously agreed upon pin number.

Need for Pin Numbers

TenPm is first and foremost a Family Wellness Tool that fosters productive communication between the parent and child. This means that use of TenPm is more of a mutual agreement between parent and child, than a kid's ball and chain. To acknowledge the child's power in this agreement, the child is a given a previously agreed upon Pin Number that, when entered into the Reply Form, will give permission to send the child's location.
